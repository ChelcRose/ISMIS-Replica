VICE PRES FOR ACADEMIC AFFAIRS OFFICE

Friday, September 6, 2024 7:01 AM

USC Days Preparations

To: Tertiary Students
It has come to our attention that some students stay beyond the last class hour/class schedule and the reason given is the preparation for the upcoming USC Days 2024. In consultation with the host schools, this is to reiterate that extended stay in the campus is not permitted, as it will pose a serious concern in ensuring the safety and security of everyone inside the campus. Rehearsing late at night, whether within or outside the campus, poses a security concern. The students are enjoined to rehearse within the official business hours , ie., up to 9:00 in the evening, and to always keep in mind their safety and well-being.

For your guidance and compliance. Keep safe, Carolinian!

(Sgd.) Atty. Joan S. Largo
Vice President for Administration
